About

A few words about me and my work.

I am an independent researcher based in Hong Kong working in mathematical category theory. My work focuses on the intersection of higher topos theory, quantum mechanics, and formal logic. I also serve as a reviewer for zbMATH Open, contributing to the evaluation and indexing of mathematical research.

I work independently and asynchronously with collaborators around the world.
