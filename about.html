About

I build explicit categorical models to make cohesion and quantum modalities concrete and usable. The emphasis is on clarity, examples, and compositionality.

Background

My research blends higher-category theory, operator-algebraic structure, and semantics for compositional systems. I prioritize constructions that can be checked and reused.

Approach

Concrete models give traction: they guide intuition, produce counterexamples, and reveal obstructions that abstract statements obscure.
