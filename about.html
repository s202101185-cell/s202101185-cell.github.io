About

A few words about me and my work.

I am an independent researcher based in Hong Kong working in mathematical category theory. My work focuses on the intersection of higher topos theory, quantum mechanics, and formal logic.

I work independently and asynchronously with collaborators around the world. My research is driven by a desire to understand the deep structures that underlie both physical reality and abstract reasoning, and to build new mathematical tools that can one day make artificial intelligence safer and more transparent.
