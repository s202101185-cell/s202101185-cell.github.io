Overview

I work at the intersection of higher topos theory and quantum foundations. My research builds concrete models of cohesive ∞‑topoi equipped with quantum modalities — making Schreiber’s programme explicit in finite‑dimensional settings and connecting category‑theoretic formalisms to quantum information structures.
