Cohesive ∞‑Topoi and Quantum Modalities

In the 2010s, Urs Schreiber proposed a synthetic language for quantum mechanics inside a cohesive ∞‑topos—a mathematical universe where discrete and continuous structures interact.

I constructed the first concrete model of this programme using finite‑dimensional C*-algebras and the inclusion of commutative subalgebras.

The Precomposition Obstruction

This degeneracy is not an accident. I proved a General Obstruction Theorem and am currently working on resolutions via mixed distributive laws.
