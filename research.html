Cohesive ∞‑Topoi & Quantum Modalities

Schreiber's programme proposes a synthetic setting for quantum and classical notions inside cohesive higher topos theory. I provide explicit, finite‑dimensional models using C*-algebras, study monoidal comonads which implement quantum modalities, and explore obstructions to precomposition and coreflection.

Current Directions

  • Resolutions of the precomposition obstruction via mixed distributive laws.
  • Connections between quantum modalities and compositional learning models.
  • Concrete constructions to support computation and visualization of modalities.
